Educational Map - Interpretive Framework

This map shows approximate biblical territories for educational and illustrative purposes. Boundaries are simplified representations based on biblical descriptions and traditional interpretations, not precise archaeological or historical claims.

This map uses a biblical maximalist framework, depicting territories as described in the biblical text. Coordinate precision is intentionally limited to emphasize the illustrative nature of the visualization.

For scholarly research, please consult academic biblical atlases and archaeological resources.
